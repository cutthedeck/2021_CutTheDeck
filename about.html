- Our Mission -

To provide users with an enjoyable virtual interface to play card games


- About -

At Cut The Deck, we love cards.  We have taken this enthusiasm and created what some are
calling the best card playing experience ever to grace the internet.  Our team, made up of three
dedicated and experienced student developers, have spared no expense and cut no corners.
You, the user, can now reap the benefits.  Please, feel free to browse our extensive collection
of more than two games, and play to your hearts content.  Thank you for visiting our site!
