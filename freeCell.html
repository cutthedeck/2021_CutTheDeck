    Rules:

    1. Cards can be moved around the 8 piles as long as the space is empty or the top card is one higher of an opposite suit.
    2. The four free cells on the left can be used to store any one card.
    3. The goal is to fill the stacks on the right hand side with a sequence of the same suit starting from ace going to king.
    4. You can move cards by dragging, or by double clicking a card to add it to its pile.
    5. While moving multiple organized cards, the amount you can move will be automatically calculate the amount that you can use based on free cells and open spaces.
    --(With 3 free cells you can move 4 cards, with 2 free cells and one open space you can move 6, etc...)
